Hi, my name is Jennifer Lindsay

But you can call me Jen.

I am a software engineer and engineering manager specializing in web and mobile application development. I currently serve as a Software Engineering Manager at Collectors, where I lead a team of Java, React, and Flutter engineers responsible for building and maintaining the platform behind the PSA Vault.

Since 2024, I have also overseen the launch of several PSA Marketplace initiatives, including the PSA/eBay Marketplace, PSA Partner Offers, and Power Packs (in partnership with GameStop).

Previously, I worked as a Software Engineering Lead at Wondery, where I helped launch the Wondery Mobile App, an exclusive listening experience for fans of the Wondery Podcast Network.

In parallel with my engineering career, I spent more than fifteen years as a freelance opera singer based primarily in the greater Los Angeles area. I also joined the Metropolitan Opera Extra Chorus for multiple seasons, appearing in 30 performances at Lincoln Center in the Grammy-winning production of George Gershwin’s Porgy and Bess. I was honored to receive an official Grammy Certificate recognizing my contribution to the cast album, which won Best Opera Recording at the 2021 Grammy Awards.

I am currently based in Orange County, California, not far from where I grew up. (No, we don't call it "The O.C." Yes, we do say "behind the Orange Curtain.") Outside of work, I enjoy taking advantage of Southern California’s coastal lifestyle and competing with friends on Duolingo to improve my rudimentary grasp of the French language.
